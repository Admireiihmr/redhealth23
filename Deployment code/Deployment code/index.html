About Collaboration

IIHMR Bangalore & RED Health: Advancing Healthcare Mobility and Emergency Response

The Institute of Health Management Research, Bangalore (IIHMR Bangalore) is a premier institution for hospital and health management education in South India. Formerly known as the Indian Institute of Health Management Research, IIHMR Bangalore has been a leader in health and hospital management education, training, and research for over a decade. Located in the heart of Silicon Valley at Electronic City Phase-1, the campus is surrounded by renowned IT and allied companies such as Wipro, HP, and Infosys, fostering an ecosystem of innovation and industry collaboration.

In partnership with RED Health, a pioneer in healthcare mobility and emergency response, IIHMR Bangalore is committed to developing highly skilled professionals in this critical field. This collaboration merges academic excellence with real-world EMS expertise, creating a transformative learning experience that equips healthcare professionals with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills.

Together, IIHMR Bangalore and RED Health aim to shape the future of emergency medical services in India, driving innovation and excellence in healthcare mobility and response.

About Post Graduate Certificate in Management - Pre-Hospital Care

It is a unique and comprehensive program designed to equip professionals with the critical knowledge and skills necessary to efficiently manage pre-hospital emergency care. It bridges clinical expertise with managerial and technical competencies, focusing on emerging challenges in emergency medical services (EMS).

It is a first-of-its-kind program launched by IIHMR Bangalore and RED Health, bringing their expertise together as a premier healthcare education university and India’s largest and only JCI-accredited EMS company, respectively. Globally, such programs are offered by universities like Harvard University and the Royal College of Emergency Medicine, UK, but there are still no parallel programs in the global south that prepare EMS professionals for leadership roles.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

A bachelor’s degree in:

  • Medicine (MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, BNYS)
  • Nursing (B.Sc. Nursing or GNM with additional qualifications)
  • Allied Health Sciences (B.Sc. in Paramedical Sciences, Emergency Care, or related fields)

Diploma holders in Paramedical or Emergency Medical Services with a minimum of 2 years of work experience in healthcare or EMS.
Work Experience (Preferred but not mandatory)
  • Experience in healthcare, emergency medical services, or related fields is desirable.
  • Fresh graduates with a strong interest in EMS and pre-hospital care are also eligible.
Age Limit Applicants should ideally be between 21-35 years of age. Exceptions can be made for experienced professionals.
Other Requirements
  • Basic Computer Skills: Familiarity with computer usage for data management and IT applications in EMS.
